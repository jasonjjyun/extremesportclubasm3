About us

HEY! Wanna experience a whole new level of excitement?

We YOLO CLUB is no doubt your best option. YOLO stands for "You Only Live Once" . We are the first WINGSUIT FLYING CLUB in Melborune and we will surely give you an unforgettable experience. .

WINGSUIT FLYING is an extreme sport similar to skydiving. In this sport, a person can fly through the air using a special wingsuit. With that "cape", you can fly through everywhere you want like a superhero. May you put on the "cape", embark on our adventure and enjoy the moment in the air together! […]
